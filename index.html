Dil ki baat… unlock karke padho

Kanishkaa, teko pata tu na mere liye bohot jada special hai — mere school time se le kar aabhi present time tak, mere jitne bhi friend rahe hain, unme sab se special tu hai… aur hamesa rahegi.
Ab na tu mera safe space ban gayi hai — wo hota hai na jise din ki har choti-badi baat batayi ja sakti hai, bina judge hone ke dar se. Agar mujhe hasna bhi hota hai kisi baat pe toh sab se pehle tu yaad aati hai.
Tu ab mere liye sukoon ban gayi hai. Main kitna bhi udaas ya gussa kyun na hoon, tujhse baat karke sab shant ho jaata hai, sab theek lagne lagta hai. Tu jab bhi mere saath rahe tab bas bolti raha kar — teri baatein sunne mein bohot mazaa aata hai.
Tu mere liye itni special hai ki main shabdon mein bata nahin sakta. Main hamesha tujhe khush dekhna chahta hoon. Teri aankhon mein aansoo aur tujhe pareshan main dekh hi nahin sakta. Bata diya kar, mat socha kar itna — main hoon 🫂
Main jaanta hoon tune cheezon aur logon ko lekar bohot suffer kiya hai. Per main hoon. Wese toh ab main bhagwan mein jyaada manta nahin — per jab baat teri aati hai toh yehi pray karta hoon: tu khush rahe hamesha, tere saare sapne pure hoon, aur apan dono aise saath rahein.
"Main khuda mein manoon na,
ek tu hi dharam mere liye."

Aur kuch alfaaz hain jo tere liye rakhe hain…
